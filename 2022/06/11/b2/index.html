四级词汇
Day1

provide 提供
provide sb with sth ;provide sth for sb ;provide against 预防，防止
The companyies provide them with enough detail to make an informed decision.

supply v/n 供应
supply ... to ... ; supply ... with ....
Team-building skiils are in short supply

offer 
offer to do sth 主动要求做某事 offer sth to sb = offer sb sth 向....提供....
The elaborate patterns were believed to offer protection from/against evil
(elaborate:adj. very complicated and detailed ;carefully prepared and organized  v. 1.to explain or describe sth in a more detailed way 2.to develop a plan ,a idea ,etc. and make it complicated or detailed;

For example: She had prepared a very elaborate meal.    
He said he was resigning(辞职) but did not elaborate on his reasons.(elaborate on)
)

render v. 给予，提供;使成为，使变得
Any assistance you can render her will be appreciated.(render sb assistance)

increase : increase by / increase to 增加了/增加到
multiply v.乘；繁殖；增长   The rats multiply rapidly as the temperature rises.
the increase/decrease in sth. 某事物的增长/减少   注意介词in

reduce v. 减少，迫使  reduce to ... 减少到 reduce ... by 减少了  reduce sb to doing sth.迫使某人做某事‘
induce v. 引诱

technology n. 科技，工艺  technique n. 技术，技巧，方法  technical adj.技术的，工艺的 

analyse/analyze v.
analysis n. pl. analyses(改i为e)

commodity n. 商品，日用品
deteriorate v. 恶化，变坏  The discussion deteriorate into a bitter quarrel.
(quarrel 拼写的时候注意后面的一个e字母，不是a ,争吵)

arise vi. (问题)出现，发生；由...引起；起来，起身
The crisis has arisen in the company.

liable :1 .(be liable for sth) legally responsible for paying the cost of sth 法律上负有偿付责任
2. (be liable to do sth ) likely to do
3. (be liable to sth) likely to be effected by sth / likely to be punished by law for sth 

potential adj.潜在的，可能的 n. 潜力
What could have been considered (as) the beginning of a descent is now a potential turnning point.
descent. n.下降 n. 斜坡
could have done :虚拟语气/主观推测/本来可以做但没有/表示责备 等等(语法可能会另外开一个模块罢)

feasible adj.可行的，行得通的
